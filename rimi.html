Problem Statement

  • Inefficient Attendance: Manual roll calls waste time.
  • Resource Mismanagement: Scheduling equipment is cumbersome.
  • Safety Gaps: Alerts rely on separate systems.

Impact

  • Optimized Resources: Reduces downtime by 30%.
  • Enhanced Safety: Instant alerts via integrated systems.
  • Active Engagement: Live quizzes boost participation.
